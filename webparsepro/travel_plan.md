Day 1:
- Morning: Arrival at Bhuntar Airport. Transfer to the hotel in Manali.
- Afternoon: Explore Old Manali. Visit the local markets, cafes, and handicraft shops.
- Evening: Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant in Old Manali.

Day 2:
- Morning: Visit Rohtang Pass. Enjoy skiing, snowboarding, and snowball fights.
- Afternoon: Have a picnic lunch at Rohtang Pass.
- Evening: Return to the hotel and relax. Optional: Explore the local nightlife in Manali.

Day 3:
- Morning: Visit Hadimba Devi Temple. Explore the temple's intricate wooden architecture and surrounding lush greenery.
- Afternoon: Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant near Hadimba Devi Temple.
- Evening: Take a leisurely walk in the nearby Van Vihar Park.

Day 4:
- Morning: Visit Solang Valley. Participate in adventure activities like zorbing, paragliding, and horse riding.
- Afternoon: Have lunch at a restaurant in Solang Valley.
- Evening: Return to the hotel and have a relaxing evening. Optional: Take a dip in the hotel's pool or enjoy a spa session.

Day 5:
- Morning: Visit Manikaran. Take a dip in the hot springs and explore the Gurudwara Manikaran Sahib.
- Afternoon: Enjoy langar (community kitchen) at the Gurudwara Manikaran Sahib.
- Evening: Return to the hotel and pack for departure. Transfer to Bhuntar Airport.

Notes:
- It is recommended to book a transportation service or hire a driver for easier and convenient travel between destinations.
- Some activities in Rohtang Pass and Solang Valley may be weather-dependent. It is advised to check the weather forecast and plan accordingly.
- Make sure to pack warm clothing and appropriate gear for snow activities.
- It is recommended to try local cuisine, especially traditional Himachali dishes, during your time in Manali.

This itinerary is designed to provide a balance between exploring the cultural and natural attractions of Manali while allowing for relaxation and flexibility in the schedule. It incorporates the customer's preferences for winter weather, mountains, exploring new cultures, and nature in its raw form while also considering the limited duration of 5 days and the need to accommodate traveling with kids.
